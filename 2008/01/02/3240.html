Jasper on Food Network

Better late than never….

A friend of Gay’s from college, Jasper Alexander, was on The Food Network last year with Bobby Flay. Jasper and his wife Beth used to live in Seattle and we went to their wedding back when Gay and I were first dating. He worked at Seattle restaurants before moving to Saratoga Springs, New York several years ago to open their own restaurant, Hattie’s Restaurant. Congratulations, Jasper!
