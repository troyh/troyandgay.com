Real entrepreneurs don't raise venture capital

Weblogs, Inc founder, Jason Calacanis says that real entrepreneurs don’t raise venture capital. I agree. Build your business with your own two hands, it’s more rewarding and fun. You learn a lot more which will help you in the later years of your business.

BTW, notice the Google ads on the top of the page – they’re all for VCs. I think that proves Jason’s point.
