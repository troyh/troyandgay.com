First Recipe Site to Support RSS!

You can now get a feed of Recipezaar’s newest recipes (100 added each day) or subscribe to the new recipes in just the categories you are interested in… e.g. Newest Italian Recipes, Newest Low Carb Recipes, Newest Vegetarian Recipes, Newest Appetizer Recipes, Newest Cocktail Recipes, or Newest Low Carb Vegetarian Italian Dinner Party Appetizer Recipes etc. pick from any combination of over 200 categories.

And if you are looking for an RSS Aggregator to use to view these subscriptions, we recommend MyWireService, of course; and we offer more help with RSS on Recipezaar.

I am pretty sure this makes us the first recipe site to support RSS.
