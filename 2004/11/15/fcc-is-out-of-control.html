FCC is out of control

We know that Michael Powell’s FCC prefers to let the few media corporations control all the media in the US. And Jeff Jarvis just proved that the FCC, in general, allows the few to control what we see on TV – complaints from just three people was enough to make the FCC levy a record $1.2 million fine against Fox. No wonder networks were afraid to show Saving Private Ryan under this new FCC.
