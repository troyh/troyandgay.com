The Hardest-Living Stalk in Produce

Kitchen hero Harold McGee takes on asparagus and proves the snap method is unreliable. Dang, this is surprising, but it will speed up our summer BBQ prep. Other things I learned: half of the world’s asparagus is white – why is it so hard to find here then? – and each stalk keep growing after harvest.
