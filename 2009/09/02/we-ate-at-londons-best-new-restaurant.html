We ate at London's best new restaurant

The Gordon Ramsay restaurant, Murano, that we went to when we were in London was just voted London’s best new restaurant by Harden’s London Restaurants Guide. It’s definitely one of the top 3 dining experiences I’ve ever had, if not the best. It’s too bad that Seattle doesn’t have a restaurant of that caliber for special occasions.
