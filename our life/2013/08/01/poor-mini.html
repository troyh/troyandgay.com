Poor Mini

Several weeks ago, just a few weeks after we got it, Gay broke the Mini when she opened her door in a residential neighborhood and a car ran right into the door, bending it forward. Only the Mini and the other car were hurt, fortunately.

It’s been in the repair shop ever since waiting on a special tool, called a Celette, that BMW requires to repair damage to the frame. We still don’t know when it will be done.
