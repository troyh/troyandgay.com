    Today’s award for good use of internet: The Darwinian Poetry site. In an experiment, this guy has taken 1,000 random words and creates random poems out of them. You vote between two which poem is better, and he slices the better poem with a new one to make a superior poem. It will take a hundred of us monkies to see if the computer can eventually create a good poem. So far the only measurable result is that poems with the word “ship” in them do better.
