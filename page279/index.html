    Wi-Fi Ferries

    Our ferries are getting Wi-Fi. Unfortunately, not the Vashon ferry yet. Whenever I see stuff like this, I laugh at all the people who think that the “offline scenario” is so critical. There’s companies and people working on making internet technology work well when you’re not connected. Here’s a prediction: most of us will be connected to the internet in most places, and when we’re not, we don’t care about the internet.
