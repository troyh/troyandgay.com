  • Sep 10, 2002

    New submissions process

    The new submissions process for recipes went into effect. Recipes submitted on the site now go into the database rather than to text files. This makes it easier to work with the submissions, makes it possible to show users the status of their submissions and even edit their submissions, although we don’t yet do that. Eventually, all submissions (reviews and photos) will work this way too.

  • Sep 9, 2002

    Ethernet wiring

    Kelly Electric came out and finished up running ethernet through the house. We now have an ethernet jack in the office and ethernet running into the crawl space, where it is running up to the stereo closet so our 802.11b access point can be centrally-located. Eventually, we’ll put a machine in there connected to an input of the stereo to stream MP3s throughout the house stereo.
