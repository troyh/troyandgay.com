What's up Tigerlily? for the WWF Set

Troy’s brother turned us on to this new show on the Spike TV network: Most Extreme Elimination Challenge. He characterized it as Jackass3, but it is much crazier than that. Apparently a real Japanese show called “Takashi’s Castle” where normal folks in incomprehensible costumes endure ridiculous and straining physical challenges (41 people have broken their ribs in the show’s 2 seasons!) is then re-dubbed with subtle and not subtle ribald humor. It sounds so stupid when I try to explain it…there is a real iron-chef-meets-beavis-and-butthead thing going on. (thurs, sat & sun at 9)
