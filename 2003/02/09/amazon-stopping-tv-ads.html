Feb 9, 2003

Amazon stopping TV ads

Telling for how bad TV ads are versus providing true value to customers, Amazon will stop running ads on TV altogether and will rely solely on word-of-mouth to drive sales: “Over the last couple of years, we have come to understand how low prices drive volume,” said Mr. Bezos, a former Wall Street trader. “We think we get a better return giving the money to customers instead of television networks.”
