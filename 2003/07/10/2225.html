Grandaddy’s Sumday is the most pleasantly fun album I’ve heard in a long time.

The line “The supervisor guy turns off the factory lights so the robots have to work in the dark” reminds me of GM’s 1980s mission to replace factory workers with robots so they, among other things, could save money on electricity in the factories.
