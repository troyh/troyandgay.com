Oct 1, 2003

Kiernan & Mike

Old friends Kiernan and Mike were out for dinner on Friday. These friends seem to be on a US tour: Seattle > New York > Seattle > San Francisco > Seattle …and they plan on moving back to SF after Mike completes his year assisting 9th circuit appellate Judge Richard Tallman. Mike used to (unfavorably) cover the Microsoft beat for The Seattle Weekly and is now characterized as the Weekly’s “most nakedly ambitious writer ever” by his ex-editor in Seattle and the Demons of Ambition – I’m as provincial as the next Seattlite, but it is an oxymoronic title…

We’ll just have to enjoy them while they are here.
