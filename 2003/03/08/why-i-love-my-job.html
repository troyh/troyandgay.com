Mar 8, 2003

Why I love MY job

Every couple of weeks someone else realizes that they have become addicted to Recipezaar. Not only do folks really get off on our caring community, but they really are cooking better, more, trying new things. I get so touched when I hear them repeat our motto too “you make Recipezaar a bigger and better place.”

And if that wasn’t all this weekend is the Dana-athon. Dana is one of our members who is battling brain cancer, and folks around the world are cooking her recipes this weekend as a way to send her good energy. What a neat idea. UPDATE: 164 people participated! I doubt I could get 164 people to cook for me…
