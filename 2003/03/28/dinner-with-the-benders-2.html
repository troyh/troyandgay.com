Dinner with the Benders

Had good friends John and Deborah Bender over for dinner and drinks – I should probably say ‘drinks with a little dinner’. Had Blue Cheese Beef Tenderloin (but with a rib-eye roast instead), Leek Risotto, a salad and Chocolate Fudge Brownies with ice cream. Boys enjoyed a narrow lead over the girls in 20th Century Trivial Pursuit before we had to call it quits.
