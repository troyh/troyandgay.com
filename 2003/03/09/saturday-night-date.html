Mar 9, 2003

Saturday night date

Went to the all-new Sound Food restaurant on the island last night for dinner. I think it was the 2nd best food I’ve had on the island; an island that is severely lacking in good restaurants. We then came home and played a close game of Trivial Pursuit and I won with an unbelievable gimmie question: “Who, after recovering from testicular cancer, wrote a book called It’s not about the bike”. HA ha.

Speaking of Lance Armstrong, he finished 32nd in the 5-stage Tour of Murcia today.
