Mar 6, 2003

Slashdot subscriptions

Slashdot is adding to their subscription offering. Now, you get access to new stories 10-20 minutes before non-subscribers. I like Slashdot and could be convinced to pay for it, but is this the best they can do for subscribers? Slashdot is late as it is. I find the news from RSS feeds and NetNewsWire long before Slashdot gets it posted, sometimes by a day or two (!) – 10-20 minutes is not a great benefit.

Why don’t they offer useful services like a summary of the week’s news along with the highly-moderated discussions posts (not everyone reads Slashdot 5 times a day), more customized news filtering, email notifications of specific articles, etc.
