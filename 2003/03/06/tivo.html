Mar 6, 2003

TiVo

TiVo announced quarterly earnings today. They’re still losing money ($21 million for the year) but they’re growing and now have 624,000 subscribers. I believe their goal is 1 million by the end of the year. I can’t wait til they ship the Home Media Option so I can view my iPhoto pics and listen to my iTunes music through TiVo.

And I wish they’d start competing with Netflix. They now have the tools to deliver almost-on-demand movies. I’d pay for that!
