Safari reviews

Crikey, there’s already several detailed reviews of [](http://www.apple.com/safari/)Safari: Mark Pilgrim’s and scroll to the bottom to find several more, including MovableType’s Mena Trott.

Also, the lead dev for Safari sent email to the KHTML mailing list announcing the open-source-ness of Safari and thanking them. It’s very cool to see big companies like Apple embracing and working with open source devs. It’s a new world.

Interestingly, many Safari devs are ex-Eazel people. And David Hyatt, the dev behind Chimera, apparently was hired more because he is the creator of XBL than for Chimera.
