Jun 11, 2003

Movie

Just finished The Transporter, an action movie with the guy from Lock, Stock and Two Smoking Barrels (which I loved) and Snatch. The plot is pretty undeveloped and oddly unexplained, but the point of the movie is the action – which is pretty good. The initial driving scene is great (in a BMW 735!) then his choice of cars plummets throughout the movie as does the action in the car scenes. Lots of fight scenes, mostly typical, but there’s a cool fight scene in oil and uses parts of a bike pretty well. Overall, entertaining but I was glad it was only 92 minutes.

Tomorrow night’s movie is XXX, which promises to be the dumbest movie since the last Steven Seagal movie.
