Jun 2, 2003

Movie

Saw Clint Eastwood’s Blood Work last night with Gay’s dad and aunts. It was good, not great. It was a straightforward detective story with a slightly different plot. But it was entertaining and Clint Eastwood is as good as usual. There were some annoying actors, like the comedian/actor Paul Rodriguez and Wanda de Jesus was too melodramatic. Jeff Daniels was good, though. I’ll always remember Jeff Daniels from Something Wild that I liked as a teenager.
