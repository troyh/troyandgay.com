MyWireService Upgrade

We’ve made some pretty significant enhancements to the MyWireService beta. We now support creating your new newspaper sections, saved clippings are more accessible, and we’ve made it easier to get to past news, and a lot more, including working around a dumb CSS redraw bug in IE that made it almost impossible to read your news. Did I mention that we added The Globe & Mail for Bergy and we’re still looking for more Canadian news sources? ;-) We actually feel good enough to start telling people about it. So if you have a news junkie or weblogging friend, tell them about it.
