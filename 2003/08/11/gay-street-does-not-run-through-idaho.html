Gay Street Does Not Run Through Idaho

We’re late-comers to Queer Eye for the Straight Guy, but we are loving it. Unfortunately the Boise, Idaho, affiliate decided to not show the latest episode. Letters to the editor to the local paper seem to make the point: Is KTVB worried that Idahoans will finally learn that the mullet and fanny-pack are fashion no-no´s? … Tell me what mother would not like to see her son get a haircut, wear nice clothes and learn to cook a decent meal?
