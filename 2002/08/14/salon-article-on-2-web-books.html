Salon article on 2 web books

Salon has an article about 2 books that claim the web is dead; one says because of lack of business, the other says because of lack of content. Of course, they’re both wrong. In this context, statements like “Web content is dead” or “AOL Time Warner will dominate” aren’t so much wrong as irrelevant. Web content is everywhere. No one can dominate the . And the Web belongs to its users. That’s not the end of a story, it’s the beginning.
