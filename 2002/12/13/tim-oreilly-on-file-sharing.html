Tim O'Reilly on file-sharing

In Piracy is Progressive Taxation, and Other Thoughts on the Evolution of Online Distribution, Tim O’Reilly explains why electronic distribution is valuable and desirable. He also predicts that subscription sites will flourish and notes that HBO, a subscription service on top of your cable (ISP) bill, is television’s most profitable network. Why? Because of content.
